Ooty

The Queen of Hill Stations! Nestled in the picturesque Nilgiri Hills of Tamil Nadu, Ooty is a charming hill station that captivates visitors with its lush green landscapes, pleasant weather, and colonial charm. Popularly known as "Queen of Hill Stations," Ooty offers a perfect retreat from the hustle and bustle of city life.

Munnar

The Land of Tea Gardens and Majestic Hills! Located in the picturesque Western Ghats of Kerala, Munnar is a breathtaking hill station renowned for its stunning landscapes, cool climate, and lush greenery. Nestled at an altitude of 1,600 meters above sea level, Munnar is a paradise for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts alike.

Kodaiknal

The Princess of Hill Stations! Nestled in the Palani Hills of Tamil Nadu, Kodaikanal is a serene and picturesque hill station that enchants visitors with its misty mountains, verdant valleys, and tranquil lakes. Known as the "Princess of Hill Stations," Kodaikanal offers a perfect escape into nature's embrace.

Wayanad

Nature's Paradise! Nestled amidst the majestic Western Ghats of Kerala, Wayanad is a captivating destination that enthralls visitors with its pristine natural beauty, lush green landscapes, and rich cultural heritage. With its mist-covered mountains, cascading waterfalls, wildlife sanctuaries, and spice plantations, Wayanad offers a delightful escape into nature's embrace.

Iduki

Nature's Abode in the Western Ghats! Idukki, nestled in the picturesque Western Ghats of Kerala, is a captivating destination known for its pristine beauty, lush greenery, and enchanting landscapes. With its sprawling tea plantations, mist-covered hills, sparkling waterfalls, and serene backwaters, Idukki offers a perfect retreat for nature lovers and adventure enthusiasts.

Mysore

The Cultural Capital of Karnataka! Located in the southern part of Karnataka, Mysuru (formerly known as Mysore) is a city steeped in rich history, vibrant culture, and royal grandeur. Renowned for its majestic palaces, ancient temples, colorful festivals, and traditional arts and crafts, Mysuru offers a captivating blend of old-world charm and modernity.
